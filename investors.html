Investor FAQ

What is the minimum investment? +
There is no platform-mandated minimum. Each startup sets their own terms, typically ranging from $10K to $100K for angel rounds.
How are startups verified? +
Every startup undergoes a rigorous verification: financial audit, founder background checks, customer interviews, and technical due diligence. Only ~15% of applicants pass our screening.
Is my information protected? +
Absolutely. All investor data is encrypted and NDA-protected. Startups cannot see your identity until you choose to connect with them.
What are the fees? +
HIT is free for investors. We charge startups a small success fee only when they successfully close funding through our platform.
